The Importance of This
Web App

In real-life scenarios, fake news can have significant consequences, shaping public perception and potentially leading to societal unrest, economic instability, or harm to individuals' reputations.

Definition of Fake News

Fake news refers to false or misleading information presented as news, often created to influence public opinion, generate confusion, or achieve specific political or financial gains.
