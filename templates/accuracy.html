How is Accuracy
calculated?

Accuracy is one of the most basic
evaluation metrics in machine learning.
It measures how often the model’s
predictions are correct.

Formula

For example, if the system correctly identified 90 real news and 85 fake news out of 200 articles, the accuracy would be:

(90 + 85) / 200 = 87.5%

While accuracy is useful, it’s often used alongside other metrics such as precision, recall, and F1-score to give a
fuller picture—especially when datasets are imbalanced.
